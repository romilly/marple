### Conway's Game of Life

Built up step-by-step to a single self-contained dfn:
```apl
life←{⎕IO←0 ⋄ s←{(1↑⍺)⊖(1↓⍺)⌽⍵} ⋄ P←(⍉3 3⊤⍳9)-1 ⋄ N←(+⌿P(s⍤1 2)⍵)-⍵ ⋄ (N=3)∨⍵∧N=2}
```
- Uses rank operator to apply 9 shift pairs, +⌿ to sum neighbours
- Shift pairs generated programmatically with ⊤ and ⍳ (not hardcoded)
- Extended encode (⊤) to support vector right argument
- Localised system variables in dfns (⎕IO←0 inside dfn doesn't leak)
- Multi-line dfn support in script runner and pico_client
- `life` workspace with glider, shift, show, and life dfns
- PRIDE video recorded
